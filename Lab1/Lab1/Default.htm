Where I'm From

I was originally born in California before moving to Spring Hill, Tennessee at the age of 4. I lived in Spring Hill for 12 years meaning I practically grew up there. Eventually, I moved to Hilton Head, South Carolina and went to high school there. It was a big change, but I love living there now.

Differences in Home Towns

Spring Hill

  • Land Locked
  • Plenty of places to camp
  • Need a car to get around
  • Plenty of restaurants and shops
  • Nothing to do

Hilton Head

  • Tons of Beaches
  • Have to go further out to camp
  • Plenty of walking paths
  • A metric ton of local restaurants and shops
  • Always something to do
